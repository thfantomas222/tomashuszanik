MORE ABOUT ME

My name is Tomas Huszanik and I am a guitarist, composer and music producer from Slovakia. Music has been a central part of my life for many years. What started as a passion for guitar gradually grew into songwriting, recording, production and building complete music projects from the ground up.

I was previously involved in the band Dennica, where I worked as a songwriter, guitarist and producer. Besides performing live, I participated in composing original material, arranging songs, recording various instruments and shaping the overall sound of the band's releases. Over the years I developed experience not only as a musician but also as a producer and recording enthusiast. I enjoy every stage of the creative process — from the first music idea to the final mix. This includes songwriting, guitar recording, editing, production, basic mixing and even visual concepts related to music releases.

Today I focus primarily on my solo project Rovina 7, a project built around melody, atmosphere and expressive guitar playing. The project draws inspiration from ambient music, post-rock, progressive rock, blues and cinematic soundscapes while remaining rooted in strong melodic ideas. I am particularly interested in projects where melody, atmosphere and musicianship matter more than trends. I believe that music does not need to be loud to be powerful.

Whether working on my own material or collaborating with others, my goal remains the same: to create honest music with lasting emotional impact.

Available for guitar recording, songwriting collaboration and music production projects.

MY MUSIC ENDEAVOURS

My music journey started with small independent projects and gradually evolved from playing guitar into songwriting, recording and music production. One of my earliest projects was the duo Absence of Existence, where I began exploring original music and collaborative songwriting.

In 2020, I co-founded the band Dennica, contributing as a guitarist, songwriter, arranger and producer. Alongside performing live, I participated in recording sessions, composing original material, arranging songs and shaping the overall sound of the band’s releases. Over time, my focus expanded beyond guitar itself. I became increasingly interested in the entire creative process — from the first idea to recording, editing, basic mixing and building complete releases.

Today I continue independently through Rovina 7, a project centred around melody, atmosphere and expressive guitar playing. Alongside original music, I actively share guitar performances and recordings on YouTube.
