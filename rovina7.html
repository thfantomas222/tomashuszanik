THIS IS IT

A STUDIO-BASED PROJECT BY TOMAS HUSZANIK

Rovina 7 is a music project where melody, atmosphere and musicianship matter more than trends. Inspired by psychedelic rock, jazz fusion and cinematic sound, the project focuses on expressive guitar-driven compositions built around mood, space and memorable musical ideas.

ROVINA 7

EVEN GROUND

2026, EP

Saudade — the Portuguese word for a longing that carries beauty in its melancholy. This EP distils that feeling into five atmospheric pieces. Sky and light filtered through half-open blinds: impressions of distance, memory, and the ache of something just out of reach. A quieter, more ambient side of Rovina 7.
